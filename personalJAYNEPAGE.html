About Me

If you've seen my page that gives an overview of Cerebellar Hypoplasia in felines you have already learned that I'm not very much of a typer, that I'm awesome and that I have a mild case of CH myself. I'm very passionate about spreading the real facts of these real kitties. I have a big job to accomplish! Even some veterinary professionals aren't aware of who we are and what we can do!
Some of the most reputable "No-Kill" shelters have pronounced us as un-adoptable. (I think you you know what that means.)
We are usually very smart cats and are generally wonderfully sweet "love bugs".
